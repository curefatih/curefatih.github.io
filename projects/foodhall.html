About this project

Foodhall is built around a simple idea: every restaurant gets its own branded space to present menus, hours, and story — while also plugging into a shared marketplace where guests browse venues the way they would in any modern marketplace app. Discovery stays centralized; identity stays yours.

On the guest side, ordering and payment stay lightweight: scan a QR, tap NFC, or follow a short link to open the bill, add items, and confirm — without juggling paper menus or counter queues. Kitchen and floor teams see updates in realtime so prep and service stay in sync.

Private venue space

Each partner runs their own menu, branding, and policies inside a dedicated hall profile.

Shared discovery

A common browse and search layer helps guests find what to eat next, similar to marketplace listings.

QR & NFC payments

Table-side and counter flows use QR and NFC-friendly handoffs so paying and reordering stay frictionless.

Realtime operations

Orders and hall activity update live for staff and customers, so everyone sees the same moment.
