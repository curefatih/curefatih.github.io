About this project

Workflow Builder is a product concept for teams that want repeatable AI workflows without giving up control. Authors compose flows in a visual editor: LLM calls, tool use, branching logic, and connectors to the channels where work actually happens — including social platforms for publishing, monitoring, or triage, with credentials and scopes managed in one place.

Not every step should run unattended. The same graph can insert approval gates and human-in-the-loop checkpoints: reviewers see context, diffs, or generated drafts, approve or reject, add notes, and only then does execution continue. That pattern fits policy-heavy launches, customer-facing copy, and anything where a person must stay accountable.

AI-native building blocks

Chain prompts, structured outputs, and tools in a canvas that stays readable as flows grow.

Social & channel integrations

Wire workflows to the networks and APIs you already use so automation stays close to real audiences and signals.

Approvals & HITL

Pause runs for review, capture decisions, and resume with a clear audit trail for compliance and trust.

Templates & deployment

Share blueprints across teams and promote versions through controlled environments when you are ready to ship.
